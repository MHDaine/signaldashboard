### How Nikhil Structures Arguments

#### The Contrarian Reframe
He opens by challenging conventional wisdom, then provides the "real" answer:
- "Most marketers think X → Here's what's actually happening"
- "Everyone overcomplicates X → The definition I love is..."
- "If you're still doing X → You're paying a 'dumb tax'"

#### The Experience-Backed Credibility
He earns the right to teach through specific proof:
- "I've managed budgets from $10K/month to $500M/year"
- "After interviewing 100+ marketers..."

Key insights are framed as discoveries:
- "That's the unlock"
- "Here's what changed everything for us"
- "The real unlock: Not all conversions are equal"

#### The Systems Thinking Frame

- "Tools vs. systems"
- "Signal vs. noise"
- "Performance is just the 'measurable subset'"
