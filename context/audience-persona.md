## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Company Characteristics

| Attribute | Ideal Profile |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Industry** | B2B SaaS, Fintech, eCommerce/DTC, Technology, Healthcare Tech—sectors with high digital acquisition costs and pressure to adopt AI |
| **Company Size** | Mid-market: 50-500 employees, $10M-$100M ARR. Venture-backed growth stage (Series A-C) also qualifies regardless of revenue |

| **Budget** | $360K+ annualized marketing services budget ($30K/mo MH-1 commitment). Typically replacing or consolidating 2-3 existing vendors |

### Buying Characteristics

| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Decision Timeline** | 2-6 weeks. "Cancel anytime" accelerates decision vs. typical 6-12 month agency commitments |
| **Buying Committee** | Champion: VP Marketing/Growth. Decision Maker: CMO or CEO. Influencer: Head of Ops/Finance. Blocker: CFO (budget scrutiny) |
| **Procurement Process** | Light procurement. Often handled directly by marketing leadership without formal RFP. May require CEO sign-off at $30K/mo |

### Problem Fit

**Primary Pain Points Addressed:**
1. **AI Adoption Gap**: Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Jasper) but not seeing EBIT impact due to poor integration and workflow chaos
2. **Vendor Fragmentation**: Managing 5+ marketing vendors/agencies, each with separate contracts, billing, and communication overhead
3. **Headcount Constraints**: Need enterprise-grade marketing capabilities but under pressure to reduce payroll and freeze hiring
4. **Speed vs. Quality Trade-off**: Traditional agencies are too slow; freelance marketplaces (Upwork) are too inconsistent

**Current Solutions They Use:**
- Multiple point solutions: Traditional agencies ($50K+/mo retainers), freelance marketplaces (Upwork, Fiverr), in-house generalists stretched thin
- AI tools without integration: Experimenting with 3-5 AI tools but manually copying/pasting between them
- Fractional CMO relationships without execution support

**Switching Triggers:**
- Just raised a funding round (Series A/B/C) and need to scale marketing quickly
- New marketing leadership (CMO/VP) looking to "reset" vendor relationships
- Board/CEO pressure to adopt AI but internal team lacks expertise
- Recent layoffs created capability gaps in marketing team
- Existing agency contract ending or underperforming

### Anti-ICP (Who NOT to target)

- **Early-stage startups (<$5M ARR)**: Can't afford $30K/mo; better served by core MarketerHire marketplace
- **Enterprise (5000+ employees)**: Prefer building internal AI teams or working with Big 4 consultancies
- **Low digital acquisition cost industries**: Manufacturing, heavy industry, real estate (traditional)—AI marketing less relevant
- **Companies seeking only creative services**: Looking for design/branding agencies, not AI-enabled growth
- **Risk-averse, slow-moving organizations**: Require 6+ month pilots and formal procurement; won't match MH-1's sprint-based model

**Objection**: "Why are we spending $30K/month on this?"

**Response Strategy**:
1. **Consolidation Math**: Show MH-1 replaces $X of current agency spend + $Y of tool subscriptions

3. **Risk Reduction**: Emphasize cancel-anytime vs. 12-month agency contracts
