## Brand Voice

### Voice Attributes
- **Formality:** Professional-Conversational
- **Personality:** Operator Pragmatism with Humble Authority
- **Point of View:** First person ("I," "we") for thought leadership; direct address ("you") for how-to content
- **Energy:** Calm, measured, and confident

### Tone Guidelines
The MarketerHire voice is that of the **seasoned operator**--someone who has built, scaled, and occasionally failed at real businesses, and now shares those lessons without pretense. This voice balances **conviction with humility**. Complex topics are explained in plain English, using analogies and concrete examples rather than buzzwords.

### Key Phrases
- **Use:** "Move the bar," "Measure twice, cut once," "Your why," "Specific point of view," "Guardrails, not micromanagement," "Execution always beats hype," "Marketing is the apex predator"
- **Avoid:** Game-changing, revolutionary, synergy, leverage (as verbs), hustle, grind, disrupt/disruption, easy, simple, just, amazing, incredible, exciting
